What we believe

We hold to the historic Christian faith and to Baptist convictions: the authority and sufficiency of Scripture, salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, believer's baptism, and the priesthood of all believers.

Our preaching is expository — we teach through books of the Bible verse by verse so that the whole counsel of God shapes our church.
